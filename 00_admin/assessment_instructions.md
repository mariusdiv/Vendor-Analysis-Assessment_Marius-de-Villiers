# Assessment Instructions

You are acting as the VP of Operations for an acquired company. Your goal is to analyze
vendor spend data, identify major cost-saving opportunities, and present your findings
- including an executive-level summary - to senior leadership.

## Deliverables

1. **Vendor Classification** (Tab: Vendor Spend Strategy)
   - Department assignment for each vendor
   - 1-line description of what the vendor does
   - Recommendation: Consolidate / Terminate / Optimize costs

2. **Top 3 Opportunities** (Tab: Top 3 Opportunities)
   - Three highest-impact cost reduction opportunities
   - Each with spend impact, action plan, and timeline

3. **Methodology** (Tab: Methodology)
   - Tools and approach used
   - Classification framework
   - Quality assurance steps

4. **Executive Memo** (Tab: CEOCFO Recommendations)
   - Summary of findings for CEO/CFO
   - Key recommendations with dollar impacts
   - Implementation timeline and risk mitigation
